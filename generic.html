Get to Know Me

I'm Caitlyn! I'm in my mid-twenties and am ready to change up my life. I've always been interested in the tech industry, especially since my dad has always worked in tech. I remember as a kid going to dad's work - Gateway. I'll never forget those cow print computers. Some of my earliest memories include playing video games, messing around on the PC, building PCs, etc. I even used to jump on my dad's World of Warcraft games at the age of five when he had to get up and go do something.

All that to say, I've been interested in this for a long time, and am extremely excited to have those dreams fulfilled. As for myself personally, I'm a fairly creative, very "geeky" kinda person. I'm an avid video-game fan, with some of my all-time favorites including Zelda, Fallout, The Witcher, and many more. I'm also quite a fan of comic books, classic art, and really good food. I have a fantastically goofy Shiba Inu named Katsu. He's five years old and the best boy. I've lived in Georgia my whole life, with the exception of that time I lived in Scotland on and off for about two years. I know what you might be thinking, and yes, it IS really that gorgeous, and yes, the accent really is hard to understand. Ask me about it!

I'm someone who likes to have a bit of fun in my every day life. I really hope to get to further shine by learning with Actualize. With the enjoyment I feel at making this website, I know I made the right choice in my future career, and I would love nothing more than to take that next step at Actualize!
